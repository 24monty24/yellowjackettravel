Dining Out

Mary Mac's Tea Room

Mary Mac's is traditional southern cooking at it's best. From the fried green tomatoes to the blackened catfish, you can't go wrong no matter what you choose off of the menu. Don't forget to ask for your complimentary "Pot Likker" on your first visit.

Paschal's Restaurant

Not only is Paschal's known for it's fried chicken and peach cobbler but it has also been a popular meeting place for the likes of Aretha Franklin, Maynard Jackson and Dr. Martin Luther King,

The Varsity

Walk a dog all the way with strings...

The Varsity is an institution! For the most pleasurable dining experience you may not want to visit when Georgia Tech football has a home game. And no matter how many other locations there are, North Avenue is the only one worth visiting.

Waffle House

The greasiest of all greasy spoons. But is there anything better than hashbrowns scattered, smothered, covered and chunked at 4 a.m. after a night of partying or studying?
